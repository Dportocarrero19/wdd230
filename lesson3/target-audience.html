Target audience & Scenarios

The targeted audience for this chamber of commerce site would be an adult either interested in business or already in a business. These adults can have some experience and would like to connect with others business-minded people. Adults who are undecided on joining a chamber can be provided the resources to make that decision using our site. The audience can also extend to interested young adults who may want to learn about business and about what a chamber of commerce is. They may still be in college with little to no education, but may need to do research on a business topic. Young adults around the college level may also be potential startup owners and would like to plan their business endeavours. The website is a great place for recent graduates to find networking events for potential employment or even contact the chamber for internship opportunities.

Personas

Frank Johnson

A 34 year old, new small business owner in the Columbia Heights area. He recently moved from another state, and is looking for information about the area. His business is a family run bakery, specializing in different types of artisan breads. He is interested in learning about the other businesses in the area, as well as promoting his business.

Caitlyn Anderson

A 21 year old college student, who is studying business at a local college in Washington DC. She has plans to start her own business after graduation, selling cosmetic products. She wants to network with people in the area, especially current business owners in Columbia Heights, as it is a major commercial area of the city. Still being in college, she hopes to look for places to find a potential paid internship before starting her business venture.
