Purpose

The purpose and objective of this site is to provide a place where the local community can gain information about the chamber of commerce. The information provided will include demographics about the area, a directory for other local business, and the benefits of joining the chamber. The chamber's own details and information will also be provided on the website for those interested in contacting.
