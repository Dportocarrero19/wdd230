Some demograhpics of the Columbia Heights area is 20.68 sq mi and holds a population of 300,000. It is located in the United States, near the Washington DC area. Many people in the city are students, and tourists.

This historic bridge is located in the outskirts to enter the columbia heights area. The bridge was constructed in 1998 and features different historical events. Many tourists love to take pictures overlooking the river and watching the many boats that travel through.

This castle building was erected by a famous wealthy donor. He was highly influential in the history of Columbia Heights and his home was turned into a museum. It holds art pieces from his time period, as well as innovations brought by other scholars. Many tourists and schools visit the building, seeing what it has to offer.

This is the residential area of Columbia Heights, which holds most of its population. There are many highrises for its populus and offers great views to see the city. The area is large and holds many different unique trees and fauna.

This district of Columbia Heights is known for its important government buildings. Many local officials live in this area. This is the area where the chamber is located. There are many buildings open to the public, although tours need to be reserved in advance.

Here is our commercial hub of the city. It is located just a few miles from the government district and holds many places to shop. This area is often bustling with people looking to purchase the latest goods. The area is famously known for its many high luxury clothing stores.
