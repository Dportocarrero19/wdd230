Select membership level of your choosing:





NP Membership

This membership is offered for non-profit companies only and is free of charge. It offeres all benefits from Gold members and below.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze membership is a great tier for new/small businesses who are looking to grow. This tier offers trainings and mentorship programs to help you with your business.The cost of the membership is $20 a month.

Silver Membership

The silver tier is the most popular tier. It grants you all the bronze tier's benefits, aswell as event discounts and invitations for exclusive networking events. The cost of the membership is $30 a month.

Gold Membership

The gold tier is an exlusive membership tier. This tier grants you both the silver and bronze tiers benefits, alongside advertising on the front page in the spotlight section. You will also be able to attend "Gold member only" chamber meetings and discuss business oppertunities with other Gold members and chamber staff. The cost of the membership is $50 a month.
