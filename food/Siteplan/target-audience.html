Target audience & Scenarios

The targeted audience for this website are those who are interested in organic, locally grown foods. They primarily provide to the local population in California, including tourists. This can be people who are trying to eat healthier and change their diet, or people who want to support their local farmers. The audience also extends to those who are also looking for fruit drinks and fruit bags. The website also targets an audience which seeks information about the southern California area. The site provides a place for those trying to get information about agricultural distribution.

Personas

Pierre Dubois

A 35 year old, French tourist who is doing research on agriculture. He wants to learn more about West Coast farming development and the delivery of agriculture throughout the area. He is interesting in contacting different companies that specialize in the distribution of food products and learning about the process.

Alicia Lopez

A 21 year old college student living in Los Angeles, who is looking to eat healthier, workout and change her diet. She is looking to start eating fruit for breakfast and wants to research different organic fruit brands. She wants to search for a company that is local to her area of living, to ensure the fruit is fresh.
