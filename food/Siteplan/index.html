Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an informational space for the Bountiful Foods business. The site will be used by users to find out what the business does and what they provide. There will also be information about the local area which Bountiful Foods operate in (California). The site will also provide a place for you to create your custom drink and see information about that drink.
